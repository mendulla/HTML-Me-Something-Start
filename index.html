My Journey with Coding

Introduction

Welcome to the story of my journey into the world of coding. This is where I share how I started and where I plan to go.

It all began at the Brown School of Washington University in St. Louis while working at the Evaluation Center as a Graduate Assistant.

First Encounter with Programming

My first programming experience involved writing SPSS syntax. It was challenging yet thrilling at the same time.

Prior to that I had been using SPSS by simply pointing and clicking on the front end. But now I was actually coding using SPSS syntax.

Projects and Practical Experience

Over the years, I've worked on various projects that have helped solidify my programming skills.

From SPSS syntax, I moved on to STATA, SAS, R, Python and now I am learning JavaScript and C# with a nonprofit organization called LaunchCode.
